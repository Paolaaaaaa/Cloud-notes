### Question 4

## AWS Well-Architected Framework Pillars
-  **security** : Managing user permissions 
- **Operational Excellence**: Continuous improvement of processes and procedures
- **Reliability**: recovery plans
- **Performance Efficiency**: Selecting the appropriate resource types and sizes based on workload requirements
- **Cost Optimization**: understanding spending and fund allocation

### Question 5


! Which benefit of the AWS Cloud adoption framework (AWS CAF) reduces operating costs, increases productivity, and improves employee and customer experience?

> **Correct Answer:**  
> -  Increase Operational Efficiency
Correct, increasing operational efficiency is a benefit of the AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (AWS CAF) that helps organizations reduce operating costs, increase productivity, and improve employee and customer experience by streamlining processes and leveraging cloud technologies.

**Why not the others?**
- **Grow Revenue**: Focuses on increasing sales and profits, not directly related to operational efficiency.
- **Reduce Business Risk**: Concerned with minimizing risks, not specifically about reducing costs or enhancing productivity.
- **Improve environmental, social, and government performance**: Addresses sustainability and governance aspects, not directly tied to operational efficiency.
