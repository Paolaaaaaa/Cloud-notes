## 4.3 Identifying AWS Technical Resources and AWS Support Options

This section covers **cost optimization tools** and **AWS support options**.

---

## Cost Optimization

### Tools to Comprehend, Measure, Control, and Optimize Costs in AWS

- **AWS Cost Explorer**
- **AWS Budgets**
- **AWS Trusted Advisor**
- **AWS Pricing Calculator**
- **AWS Cost and Usage Report (CUR)**

These tools help:
- Understand current spending
- Forecast costs
- Set budgets and alerts
- Identify optimization opportunities

## AWS Support

### AWS Support Plans Overview

| Support Plan | Response Time (SLA) | Technical Guidance | Health Dashboard | 24/7 Support | TAM | Best For |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|------------------|-------------|-----|-----|
| **Basic** | No SLA | Documentation & forums | Full | No | Shared (Pool) | All AWS customers |
| **Developer** | < 24 hours | Core checks | Basic | No (business hours) | No | Dev & test environments |
| **Business** | < 4 hours | Full checks | Enhanced | Yes | Dedicated | Production workloads |
| **Enterprise On-Ramp** | < 1 hour | Full checks | Enhanced | 24/7 | Shared TAM | Growing organizations |
| **Enterprise** | < 15 minutes | Core + proactive | Enhanced | 24/7 | Dedicated TAM | Mission-critical workloads |

| Service | Description | Cost | Use Cases |
|-------|------------|------|----------|
| **AWS Managed Services (AMS)** | AWS operates and manages your environment | Premium service | Large enterprises, critical environments, teams without operational experience |
| **AWS Partner Network (APN)** | Network of AWS-certified partners | Varies by partner | Consulting, migrations, large or complex projects |
| **AWS re:Post** | Official AWS community forum | Free | Technical questions, best practices, real-world solutions |
| **AWS Skill Builder** | Digital courses, labs, and exam prep | Free / Paid | Learning, certification preparation, skill development |
| **AWS Abuse** | Trust & Safety team for misuse of AWS resources | Free | Spam, attacks, policy violations, compromised accounts |
| **Billing Support** | Account and billing issues | Free |
